h3>What is pair programming?

Having a driver and navigator and keeping eachother from getting fatigued

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

Keep eachother in check and catch eachothers mistakes, it also fosters team enviroment.

Compare driver and navigator

    Driver
  • Write the code while listening to the navigator
  • Give feedback
  • ask questions
    Navigator
  • Look up and research code
  • Work out the logic
  • Watch the clock
