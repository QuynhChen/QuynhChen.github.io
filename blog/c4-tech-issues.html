Where are the ladies at?
One problem facing the tech world is the lower percentage of female software programmers.10/28/14
Dear Fellow Beginners,

The Problem of a skewed male to female ratio

The other day, I went to Google Headquarters for a networking tech event. I noticed that at every table were men. Splattered here and there in scarce amounts were solo females. Here's a great article on how one female dev felt after over 15 years in this depressing environment. The loneliness of a female coder The internet, namely Wiki, has rumoured that in 2013, females made up of about 20% of all US computer programmers. Currently, Google has published that females make up 30% of their workforce. Google Diversity - not.

The possible solution?

Plenty ways to approach this. Personally, I think the best way is to create welcoming environments by keeping open communication and feedback. Perhaps encourage female to female and female to male interactions.
