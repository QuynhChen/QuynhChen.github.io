Margin, Border and Padding.
What is the difference between Margin, Border and Padding? 10/8/14
Dear Fellow Beginners,

Margin

Margin is the space between an element and other elements. For example, you can change the margin to move each div around or away from another div.

Border

Border is the outline of surrounding an element. When you set border to be visible, it forms a box around your text. General, you set the Border to visible by selecting thickness of the border (1x, 2x), the type of line (solid, dashed), and color (black, blue, etc.)

Padding

Padding is the space between the text inside the box and the border. So if you don't want the text to be squished so close to your border, just increase your padding. In other words, the spaces around the text increases and the border may move away from the text.
