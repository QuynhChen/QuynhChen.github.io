Game analogy

Let's convert my personal values into a game with three variables. Variable one (challenges) initiates the player's energy bar, variable two (community) adds energy to the player's energy bar, and variable three (inner strength) is the SUPER mode when the energy bar is full. When I'm in SUPER mode, I can defeat anything, including the dark warlord "Inferior" ( Stereotype Threat). But when the energy bar is not being added to (low energy or empty bar), I'm merely alive in a game of challenges. Not dead, not amazing, just alive in the game.

One can imagine that merely being alive may not defeat one as mighty and as consuming as the dark warlord "Inferior." So ultimately, I cannot confidently say that merely thinking of my values will mediate my stereotype threat if any will exist in DBC in the future.
